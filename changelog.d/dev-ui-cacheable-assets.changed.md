- Dev-UI pages (`/wheels/info`, `/wheels/routes`, `/wheels/migrator`, etc.) no longer inline ~1MB of JS/CSS (jQuery, Semantic UI, marked, highlight.js, base64 icon font) into every response. Bundled assets are now served from a `/wheels/assets/*` route with `Cache-Control: public, max-age=31536000, immutable` and a framework-version cache-buster, shrinking typical dev-UI page payloads from ~1.1MB to under 100KB after first load ([#2959](https://github.com/wheels-dev/wheels/issues/2959)).
