ColdFusion on Wheels version 1.1 Beta 2 is now available for [download](https://cfwheels.org/). The core team and community have worked together over the past few weeks to identify and fix bugs and make this awesome new feature set more stable. Thanks again to everyone for making this project so enjoyable to work with. We’re getting very close to being production-ready on this, so be sure to give it a try and provide us with your feedback. I have been blogging about some of the new features of Wheels 1.1. Be sure to check them out and subscribe to my blog for more. :)

-   [Add to the Flash with redirectTo()](http://www.clearcrystalmedia.com/pm/add-to-flash-redirectto-cfwheels-1-1/)
-   [New assetQueryString setting](http://www.clearcrystalmedia.com/pm/new-assetquerystring-setting-cfwheels-1-1/)
-   [Experimenting with flashMessages()](http://www.clearcrystalmedia.com/pm/experimenting-flashmessages-cfwheels/)
-   [Playing around with automatic validations](http://www.clearcrystalmedia.com/pm/automatic-validations-wheels-1-1/)
