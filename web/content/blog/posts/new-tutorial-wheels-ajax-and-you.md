[Raúl Riera](https://cfwheels.org/user/profile/3) and I collaborated on a new tutorial in the reference guide called [Wheels, AJAX, and You](https://cfwheels.org/docs/chapter/wheels-ajax-and-you). The tutorial discusses 3 different ways to request and receive data from the controller using asynchronous JavaScript. While much of this may be standardized in future releases of Wheels, this tutorial gives you some fairly simple options for integrating AJAX functionality into your Wheels 1.0.x applications _today_.
