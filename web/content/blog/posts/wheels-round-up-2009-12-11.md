## Worth Noting

Worth noting is Ray Camden [blogs about FW/1](http://www.coldfusionjedi.com/index.cfm/2009/11/28/Framework-One). Ray mentions [FW/1](http://fw1.riaforge.org/) using [conventions over configuration](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Convention_over_configuration) (CoC) which is great. Wheels uses CoC, though Wheels is not as young as FW/1. Wheels has been being developed since July 2006 and recently released the [first production ready version](https://cfwheels.org/blog/index.cfm/2009/11/24/Wheels-10-Were-Production-Ready). To see the history of Wheels development through the years [click here](http://github.com/rip747/cfwheels/graphs/impact).

## Honorable Mentions
